Robotics and Embedded Sytems program objectives

Objective 1

Design and complete robotic and emedded systems solutions that apply to real-world situations and challenges

Objective 2

Implement a simple microsprocessor using digital logic design.

Objective 3

Demonstrate embedded system design skills, including, but not limited to, microcontroller selection, schematic design, printed circuit board layout, design for electromagnetic compatiblity, and design for manufacturing.

Objective 4

Apply knowledge of transducers, actuators, and simulataneous hardwar and sofware development in the design of an embedded system.

Objective 5

Design and analyze real-time embedded systems, including advanced digital logic design, signal processing and high-speed digital systems.
